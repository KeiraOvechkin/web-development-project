About Me




I am a junior at Kennesaw State Univeristy. I'm majoring in IT with a concentration in Cyber Operations Security. I am also a huge fan of graphic design and have been designing since I was a freshman in highschool. I've made book pages, posters, school newspaper articles and logos. My favorite type of designing is illustrations.
I participated in collaborative events with a team of 13 designers to make sure our design elements and styles were aligned. I also worked closely with over 10 authors of the newspaper pages to ensure their intended message was effectively conveyed through the design. The diverse range of software application I've proficeintly handled includes Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign.
I'm fairly well versed in the programming languages Java and Python. Relevent course work I've taken in regards to my major has been Programming Problem Solving, Programming Principles, Professional Development & ENT, Hardware & Software Concepts, Discrete Struct Comp, and Database Systems.
